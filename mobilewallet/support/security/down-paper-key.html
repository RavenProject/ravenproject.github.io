Why do I need to write down my paper key?

The RVN Wallet connects directly to the cryptocurrency networks, where your wallets are stored. The "keys" to access your wallets are securely stored in your phone and nowhere else. The RVN Wallet will provide you with a unique "Paper Key," which is a list of 12-words which can be used to recreate the keys to your wallet. If you ever lose or upgrade your phone, this "Paper Key" is required to regain access to your funds. Please write down each word, in order, on a piece of paper. We recommend storing your Paper Key in a safe, safety deposit box at a bank, or wherever you might keep passports, birth certificates, or other important documents. Anyone who has your Paper Key can access your money, even if they don't have your wallet PIN, so keep it private and don't show it to others!
