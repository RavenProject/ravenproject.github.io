How do I get started?

The RVN Wallet connects directly to the Ravencoin network. There are no accounts or usernames with the RVN Wallet. Instead, you will have a "wallet" where you will store your money. It's just like the physical wallet you put your cash in, except that it exists only on the internet and holds digital money.

If this is your first time using the RVN Wallet, simply choose Create New Wallet to start. Enter a PIN Confirm the PIN The app shows you 12 different words in separate pages. This is your Paper Key. Tap Write Down Paper Key and Next after writing down each word. IMPORTANT: Make sure that you write the Paper Key down in the correct order and keep it secure as this is the only way to restore your RVN Wallet. For more information about the Paper Key, visit this page. The app will ask you to confirm random words from your Paper Key. Click Submit after entering the requested words. The wallets sync to the blockchain You will see a box asking you to enable Touch ID. This is optional. Tap on X if you would like to skip this step.
