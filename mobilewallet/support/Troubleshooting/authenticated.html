Why do I get an “Insufficient funds” error when sending Ravencoin?

Your RVN Wallet needs to be connected to the internet to send and receive funds as well as to sync to the blockchain. A “No internet connection found” error means your phone does not have a stable connection to the internet, or your network connection is blocking access to the cryptocurrency networks. The best way to solve this is to connect to a strong and stable internet connection. Try the following if you encounter this error: Force close the app and try again; If you are using mobile data, switch to wifi and vice versa; Move to a different location in your house or office to get a better signal; Try again using a different internet connection in a different location.
